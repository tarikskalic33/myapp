# Explicitly Prohibited (T0-T2 Production)

PROHIBITED:
- hidden mutable global state
- unverifiable confidence claims presented as calibrated certainty
- silent semantic reinterpretation of frozen contracts
- dynamic self-modifying execution without K-bound enforcement
- runtime authority escalation without registry sealing
- audit/replay bypasses or implicit mutation pathways
- Date.now() in core logic (event-derived time only)
- array.length as sequence authority
- Set/Map in ProjectionState (arrays only)
- JSON.stringify for integrity-critical hashing (RFC 8785 only)
- Hoeffding bounds in gate logic (Bernstein only)
- V4/V5 verifier outputs entering VCG calibration loop

NOT PROHIBITED:
- T3-T5 research into compositional emergence of artificial general cognition
- spectral stability control, kappa* optimization, phase diagram analysis
- recursive self-reference over persistent event substrate
- martingale-constrained adaptive evidence accumulation

Research claims must remain tier-classified. T0-T2 contracts remain mechanically enforceable.
