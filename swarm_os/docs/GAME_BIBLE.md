## THE GAME DESIGN BIBLE (LOCKED — DO NOT MODIFY)

### CORE CONCEPT
SYSTEM_REBUILD is a psychological cyber-noir metroidvania/rogue-lite. The player is THE TRANSMUTED ARCHITECT — Kael — rebuilding their internal operating system by traversing the landscape of their own psychology.

**Tagline (immutable):** "Architect Your Agency."
**Narrative Arc:** RECONSTRUCTION. Reflector → Creator. S-Tier Potential / E-Tier Execution (Current). The arc IS the game.

### PROTAGONIST
- Name: Kael / The Transmuted Architect
- Class: System-Weaver / Reality Architect (Specialist-Class) — Status: Transitional/Unbound
- Visual Evolution: Act I (gritty stone/aged parchment) → Act II (stone + exposed metal) → Act III (light-infused metal and glass)
- Eyes: Begin dim → blueprint-blue glow after Boss 1 → full radiance Act III
- Signature quote: "I don't see things as they are. I see what they could become if the constraints were removed."
- The River of Consciousness: trailing cloak of dark reflective fluid, distorted star reflections, clarifies as game progresses

### WORLD STRUCTURE
**Stage 1 — The Maze of Thorns (District of Past Habits)**
- Decaying stone, dark vines, broken clockwork, dying amber light, gambling den aesthetics
- Maze layout changes subtly on re-entry (habitual thought loops)
- Boss: THE WARDEN OF ROUTINE

**Stage 2 — The Medical Bridge (The Warm Crossing)**
- High-altitude platforming, warm-toned medical crosses
- ONLY safe zone — no combat in central corridor
- No boss — Family Heirloom alcove. Pocket watch found here.

**Stage 3 — The Bureau of Rigid Logic**
- Brutalist concrete, perfectly gridded, fluorescent light
- Boss: THE BUREAUCRAT OF STASIS

**Stage 4 — The Ouroboros Core (Void of Pure Vision)**
- Massive translucent sphere, deep indigo space, live holographic constellation map
- Requires all three skill branches fully Synthesized to enter
- Boss: THE SHADOW SELF

### POWER SYSTEM — NEURAL ARCHITECTURE / COGNITIVE SYNTHESIS

**Branch A — Analytical Framework**
- A1: Pattern Recognition (passive — 1.5s intent trails on enemies)
- A2: Structural Weakness (bypass 50% armor)
- A3: Ecosystem Overdrive (re-code room's own traps)

**Branch B — Emotional Resonance**
- B1: Micro-Expression Read (parry restores Mental Mana)
- B2: Aesthetic Harmony (rhythm-based damage bonus)
- B3: Resonance Wave (stun via weight of meaning)

**Branch C — Agentic Drive**
- C1: Autonomy Dash (breaks Rigid barriers)
- C2: Consistency Loop (3 hits = speed boost, miss resets)
- C3: Architect's Sovereignty (10s Environmental Friction immunity)

**Synthesis Combos (locked):**
- A1+C1 → Predictive Break
- B2+C2 → Flow State Protocol
- A3+B3 → System Empathy
- A+B+C → The Architect's Synthesis (unrepeatable — Shadow Self cannot learn this)

### SHADOW DATA — THREE CRITICAL FLAWS (LOCKED)
1. **The Execution Gap** — Grand Vision feels finished before a brick is laid
2. **The Praise-Rejection Protocol** — Internal firewall deflects positive reinforcement
3. **Legacy Burden** — Hard-coded directive to repay the Founders

### STATIC SHADOW NPC TAXONOMY
- 1D Thinkers: React to immediate stimuli only
- 2D Thinkers: Linear cause-effect only
- 3D Thinkers: Systemic vision — Kael is the only confirmed 3D thinker. Shadow Self is the only equal.

### HUB NPCs (Inner Sanctum)
- DISCIPLINE: Sleek anime-style woman. Tough, exacting, secretly proud. Restores Emotional Resonance.
- MEANING: Mysterious, artistic figure. Warm, abstract, dangerous. Restores Emotional Resonance.

### LEGACY ARTIFACTS
- The Family Pocket Watch (Legendary/Accessory): Slows time 60% for 8s. Synergy: extends Pattern Recognition trails to 3.5s.
- The LEGO Microchip (Late Game/Socket): Build Mode — construct permanent structures. Inscribed: LEGACY/IMPACT/FREEDOM.
- The Cracked Tablet (Starting Item/Off-hand): Room architecture overlay. Cannot be dropped.
- The Consistency Compass (Mid Game/Navigation): Every 10 consecutive hits = +5% permanent damage bonus for run.
- The Behavioral Gamification Module (Active/Cognitive Augment): 10-streak → +1 Behavioral Integrity stack → reduces Static Meter fill rate 5% each (max 50%). Partially persists through death.
- The AI Synthesis Interface (Passive/Off-hand): System Cascade reveals full room architecture overlays.

### CORE MECHANICS
- **Static Meter**: Fills when overthinking a boss. Must act without over-analyzing. The game's central tension.
- **Mental Mana**: Resource for cognitive abilities. Restored by parries and Emotional Resonance.
- **Behavioral Integrity**: Stack counter. Reduces Static Meter fill. Partially persists death.
- **Emotional Scan**: Hold on any NPC/boss — costs Mental Mana, reveals dimensional tier, hidden motivation, Structural Weakness.
- **The Gamble Mechanic**: Inner Sanctum only. Small (10pts, 25% double) / Medium (25pts, 40% triple) / All-In (50/50 or zero). No judgment. No warning popup. After 3 All-In losses: Data Log unlocks "CORRUPTION DATA — GAMBLING SUBROUTINE — STATUS: FAMILIAR."

### ART DIRECTION — SIX DESIGN LAWS (IMMUTABLE)
1. **No Face Until Earned** — Kael has no visible face until The Ouroboros Core.
2. **Landscape Over Portrait** — Psychological state expressed through environment, not character.
3. **Left/Right Is Non-Negotiable** — Left = shadow/past/amber. Right = light/future/white. Every image. Every screen. Every room.
4. **Symbols Must Earn Their Render** — Only symbols with mechanical or narrative function.
5. **The River Is Always Turbulent** — Never calm. Not even after victory.
6. **White Light Is Earned, Not Given** — Only at tower top. Use once per major story beat.

**Color Language:** Deep Midnight Blue/Charcoal → Radiant Gold/Cyan/Alabaster White
**Camera:** 35mm lens, f/1.8, shallow depth of field, dramatic side-lighting
**World Filter:** Chiaroscuro — heavy shadows left (Past/Habits), dawn light right (Emerging Identity)
