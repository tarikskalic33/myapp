# Code of Conduct

AEGIS-- is a technical community for deterministic, replay-verifiable AI governance systems. Contributors are expected to keep discussion rigorous, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption of technical discussions.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem harmful to the community.

To report an issue, follow the security and contact guidance in `SECURITY.md` when appropriate, or contact the maintainers listed in `CODEOWNERS`.
